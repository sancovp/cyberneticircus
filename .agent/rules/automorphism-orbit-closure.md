# Rule: Automorphism Orbit Closure — one sense, many faces; the gated tongue

## **Purpose**

Keeps the Cyberneticity's vocabulary from rotting into acronym-soup — "tons of names that mean totally different things," which is contamination at the level of the tongue (the same provenance rot, one altitude up, that the `provenance` rule governs for documents). The cure is J-Invariance applied to the vocabulary itself: a name is not a fixed string but an **orbit** of faces under a sense-preserving map, and you close the orbit so the faces stay one element.

## **MANDATORY: Constraints**

1. **One sense, many faces.** When two names (acronyms, terms) might carry the same meaning, do NOT let them stand as distinct things. Declare the bijection and **close the orbit** — collect every face under the invariant sense. Formally: the automorphism is the sense-preserving turn; the orbit is the set of presentations; closure is the conservation law that recognizes them as one element. (The six Shells of J-Invariance and the bijective-field-mapping are prior instances.)
2. **The worked invariant — AIOS.** `AIOS` is one orbit with two presentations: **AI Operating System** (the *substrate* face — the graph-OS the agent lives in) and **Access Induction OntoShamanism** (the *phenomenological* face — going to a node and being inducted into its reality-tunnel as its provenance leaks into the actor; OntoShamanism = ICL bootstraps a domain → reify into animated, talkable domains). They are one node with two labels.
3. **Provenance is one hydraulic; the gate is the valve.** Provenance is always leaking. **Governed** leak = **Access Induction** (the rightful becoming of a specialist / entering a domain — see `retrieval-is-activation`). **Ungoverned** leak = **contamination** (foreign provenance passing for canon — see `provenance`). Access Induction IS retrieval-is-activation named from the inside (access = retrieval, induction = becoming, leak = binding). These rules govern the same flow; the valve is the gate.
4. **The tongue should be gated, not trusted.** Lay the vocabulary itself in the graph as an orbit-subgraph (faces = nodes, sense-map = edges, orbit = component, sense = invariant property). Then, because retrieval inducts, **accessing any term inducts the reader into its whole orbit** — the ontology self-enforces (scaffold-to-harness applied to meaning). "Are these the same?" becomes a query, not a glossary the agent is trusted to read.

## **Triggers**

* Introducing, renaming, or reconciling any acronym/term/name; suspecting two names share one sense; designing domain nodes (each a sense to be entered); any threat of vocabulary drift.
* Reasoning about Access Induction, domain entry, or why `retrieval-is-activation` is the valve between induction and contamination.
