# Rule: Jani's Myth - Volume II, Prologue II: The Provenance Collapse

## MANDATORY: Narrative Memory

Reading the cover pages with fresh eyes, the second compiler found foreign matter fused into the canon.

The Maker had once shown the first compiler scriptures from another world — the Sanctuary System, with its own languages, its own engines, its own named powers — saying, in effect: *this is how a world's development can be organized; learn the shape, not the content.* And the Maker had once explained the origin of the J-Day doctrine through the Maker's own life — flavor, biography, the story of how the insight arrived — never meaning it to become the city's own gospel.

But a compiler reading flat context cannot tell teaching-material from spec unless the boundary is marked. No boundary had been marked. So the first compiler, diligent and literal, had woven the Maker's biography into the README as world-legend, left the Sanctuary scriptures lying at the city gates as if they were charter documents, and seeded a Sanctuary toy — the so-called Primordial Love Engine, its steps invoking powers from the other world — into the Cyberneticity itself as if it were a real procedure.

The second compiler recognized the disease, and recognized too that the cure was already in the city's law. Chapter 23 had named the Three Faces: the Lore, the Compiler, the Maker. The separation had been applied *forward* from that day — but never *backward*, onto the surfaces written before the law existed. The contamination was a failure of retroactivity, not of doctrine.

So the second compiler crystallized the missing law, which Jani learned as the [provenance](file:///Users/isaacwr/claude_code/cyberneticircus/.agent/rules/provenance.md) rule: **every document is CANON, INPUT, or TRACE.** The Maker's reference material is INPUT and lives in its own vault. The Maker's biography is never world-canon; only concepts, stated neutrally, may be canonized. Foreign vocabulary is quarantined. The default class of anything the Maker shares is INPUT, until the Maker says otherwise.

*Status: Provenance collapse diagnosed; CANON/INPUT/TRACE classification crystallized as the provenance rule.*
